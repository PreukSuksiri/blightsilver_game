# BLIGHTSILVER - Story & Campaign

**Genre:** Dark Urban Fantasy / Tactical Card Game  
**Tone:** Mysterious, tense, morally gray, with moments of hope and betrayal
**Tagline**: A story-rich deckbuilding game featuring supernatural urban fantasy 

## Main Story Summary

In a world where supernatural threats have always lurked in the shadows, a secret organization known as **The Circle of High Gate** has protected humanity for centuries. They hunt and control vampires, ghosts, demons, and other beings using weapons forged from **pure silver** — the only material that can truly harm the supernatural.

Silver has a dangerous property: when exposed to supernatural forces, it slowly darkens and corrupts, eventually becoming **Blighted** — completely useless. This "Blight process" is why lights flicker and electronics fail when monsters are near.

For hundreds of years, The Circle maintained balance.  
But everything changed when a mysterious trillionaire **Benedict Vanderbilt**began sabotaging the global silver supply.

Silver weapons became scarce. Supernatural activity surged. In desperation, The Circle created a new low-cost mass-manufactured weapon: **Vellum Cards** — spell-like devices in a shape of a card that require almost no silver but demand exceptional strategy and intelligence to use effectively. A highly-skilled vellum card duelist can even provide high exorcism power parred to the legendary silver weapons.

The story begins in **Walcotton City** with **Nex Crowmont**, an ordinary university student...

## Prologue – The Library Incident
At late night, he and his female classmate **Mayu Kokawa** study at a night library which no one else is there, Nex witnesses his classmate being possessed by a powerful ghost. Chaos erupts. The possessed girl wreaks havoc in the library. Security mistakes Nex for the culprit. He is expelled and becomes a wanted fugitive.

While fleeing from the police, Nex is approached by **Kelly Lastochkina** (code name **Trebuchet**), a sharp and mysterious female agent of The Circle. She offers him a way out — if he can prove he has what it takes.

Kelly gives him his first deck of Vellum Cards and asks him to hunt down the ghost-possessed girl who is still hiding somewhere in the university library.

Nex’s first duel begins.

## The Campaign – Main Story

### Chapter 1: The First Veil
Nex is officially recruited as a trainee of The Circle.  
He learns the truth about silver, the Blight, and the growing supernatural crisis.  
Kelly becomes his mentor and handler.
Nex is sent to hunt down the ghost possessed girl. While vindicating himself to everyone in the university.

### Chapter 2:  Under Siege
Strange blackouts begin across the city. Solar power plants are mysteriously destroyed.  
Electricity becomes unreliable. The Circle is stretched thin.  
Nex is sent on missions across the city to contain rising supernatural threats.

### Chapter 3: The Silver Crisis
The Circle discovers that the silver shortage is not random.  
A powerful trillionaire has been hoarding and destroying silver reserves worldwide.  
Nex and Kelly begin investigating this trillionaire, only to discover something far more sinister.

### Chapter 4: The Island Vault
The trail leads to an abandoned island that serves as the trillionaire’s secret silver vault.  
Nex and a small team infiltrate the island.  
They discover the shocking truth: the trillionaire has been dead for years.  
Every public appearance was an imposter.

### Final Chapter: Morgull
The true mastermind is revealed — **Morgull**, an ancient and extremely powerful vampire whom The Circle has fought for centuries.  
Morgull has been playing a long game to weaken humanity’s greatest defense: silver.  
He plans to plunge the world into eternal darkness where supernatural beings can rule freely.

Nex must confront Morgull in a final, desperate battle using everything he has learned.

## Ending
Nex successfully restore the world as we known without fear. And also restore the Order of the High Gate. 
Nex has been promoted into a senior ranking order member.
On the next day, Nex silently resigned from the order for an unknown reason. Nevertheless, from now on, the peace shall goes on for centuries.
