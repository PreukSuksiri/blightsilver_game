# BLIGHTSILVER - Campaign Stages & Rewards

**Campaign Style:** Lite Final Fantasy Tactics-inspired (Node-based)  
**Total Length:** Prologue + 4 Chapters + Final Chapter (exactly 5 stages per chapter)  
**Story Structure:** Single linear story with one definitive ending  
**Progression:** Player moves from stage to stage on a world map. Each stage = one 5x5 grid duel using Vellum Cards.

## General Reward Rules
- After every stage: Small reward (Credits + minor unlocks)
- After completing a full Chapter: Major reward (Credits + new powerful Vellum Cards + permanent upgrades)
- Rewards are permanent across the entire campaign.
